Keyboard operation:

  • ARROW: Move
  • Z: OK/fire
  • SHIFT: Slow down
  • ESC: Reset

Gamepad operation:

  • D-Pad: Move
  • A: OK/fire
  • X: Slow down
  • Y: Reset

Sounds:

  • music (CC0)
  • shot sound (CC0)
  • explosion Sound (CC0)
